What MentorMe is all about

Mentor Me lets you lend your business skills to entrepreneurs across the globe. New entrepreneurs often have a lot of questions, and don't always have a mentor to ask. As an entrepreneur, whenever you need business advice, our volunteers are happy to help.

Ask Questions

By posting your questions, you can communicate directly and solve a problem. As an experience business owner you can help pay it forward just by installing the Be My Mentor app.

Build your Knowledge

By asking questions and getting different answers, only increases your overall knowledge. The more you know the more valuable to your organization you will become.

Help others grow

When you share your knowledge with others, you are teaching them to think differently. This will help them grow just as you have, while also making you a better teacher and leader.
